## Best Practices for Portfolio Project Titles

### Effective Patterns:
- **Challenge + Action + Outcome**: "Reducing appointment no-shows through automated reminders"
- **Action + Context**: "Redesigning the checkout experience for mobile users"
- **Outcome-focused**: "Building trust in telehealth: A HIPAA-compliant video platform"
- **Problem-solution**: "Simplifying complex financial data for first-time investors"

### What Makes a Strong Title:
- Starts with an action verb or outcome
- Includes the domain/context (healthcare, fintech, etc.)
- Hints at the design challenge or impact
- Avoids jargon unless industry-appropriate
- Balances specificity with intrigue

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid:
- Too vague: "Mobile App Design"
- Too technical: "Implementing WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance in React Component Library"
- Too long: More than 15-18 words
- Buzzword-heavy: "Leveraging AI-driven synergistic solutions..."
